About Us

Parul University : Projections 2022

Projections is an International Technical Fest hosted by Parul University that includes over 45 different technical, non-technical, and special events, workshops, and guest lectures. It is Gujarat's largest technical event, with over 8000 attendees and 20000 viewers. Projections'21 centred on emerging technologies from around the world and how they can help us achieve a better future. This year, in Projections'22, we'd like to talk about how people have innovated, how technology has advanced, and how mankind has evolved as a result of technological advancement.
